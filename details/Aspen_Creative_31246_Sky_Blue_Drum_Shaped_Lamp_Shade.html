Elegant Drum-Shaped Lamp Shade in Sky Blue

This drum-shaped lamp shade brings a refined touch to your lighting setup. Crafted from high-quality wrinkled parchment paper in a beautiful sky blue color, it produces a soft and diffused light that creates a calming ambiance in any room. The addition of a brass spider fitter ensures a secure and stylish connection to your lamp harp, making installation effortless and enhancing the overall aesthetic of your lighting fixture.

Perfect for table lamps, accent lamps, vintage lamps, and even kid's room lamps, this shade is designed to blend functionality with elegance. It features a balanced 8\" wide design that complements classic and modern interiors. The premium virgin styrene backing board offers durability and resilience, ensuring it maintains its shape and color over time without cracking or yellowing.

Whether you're looking to update your living room, bedroom, office, or any other space, this lamp shade provides both visual appeal and practical performance. Add a touch of timeless beauty to your lighting choices with this versatile and sophisticated addition.
