Elegant Black Pleated Linen Lamp Shades

Enhance your lighting with these beautifully crafted 2-pack lamp shades. Made from premium black linen fabric, they bring a touch of sophistication and elegance to any room. The soft pleated design adds visual interest and diffuses light to create a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Each shade features a sturdy brass clip-on fitter, making installation effortless without the need for screws. Perfect for chandeliers, candelabra lamps, and wall sconces, these shades offer a seamless fit with torpedo bulbs and deliver long-lasting durability.
