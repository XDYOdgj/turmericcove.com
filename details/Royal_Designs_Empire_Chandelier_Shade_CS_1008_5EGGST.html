Elegant Chandelier Shade with Eggshell Stripe Pattern

The Royal Designs Empire Chandelier Shade CS-1008-5EGG/ST perfectly combines vintage elegance with contemporary appeal. This timeless piece features a subtle yet sophisticated eggshell stripe design that enhances the look of any chandelier, adding a refined touch to your space.

Crafted for Style and Durability

The true bell shape of the shade ensures a balanced and elegant appearance from every angle, making it an ideal choice for both residential and commercial settings. Made from hand-tailored silk-type shantung fabric, this chandelier shade exudes a sense of luxury, blending soft texture with an upscale aesthetic.

A premium off-white styrene lining further enhances the visual appeal, providing a smooth surface that allows light to diffuse evenly. This not only improves the brightness of the chandelier but also ensures a long-lasting, high-quality finish.

Easy Installation, Hassle-Free Setup

The clip-on design of the shade makes it simple to install and remove, fitting securely over a candelabra bulb base. Whether you're updating a dining room chandelier or adding a touch of luxury to a foyer, this shade offers a versatile and elegant solution that enhances your lighting setup effortlessly.

With a durable 1.5mm metal frame, the CS-1008-5EGG/ST is built to last and maintain its beauty over time. It's a perfect addition for anyone who values both style and functionality in their lighting accessories.
